StudySmart Online

Website Planning Document

Site Name

StudySmart Online

The name was selected because it clearly communicates the goal of the website: helping students study more effectively using smart strategies and resources.

Optional domain availability: studysmart-online.com

Site Purpose

StudySmart Online provides academic support resources for students, including study tips, subject guides, productivity tools, and a contact form for tutoring.

Scenarios

  • How can I improve my study habits for online classes?
  • Where can I find tips for preparing for exams?
  • How do I contact someone for extra academic help?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color (#7F0799): Headings, header, footer
  • Secondary Color (#f4f7fb): Page background
  • Accent Color (#AE76A6): Borders and highlights

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Headings
  • Roboto: Body text
